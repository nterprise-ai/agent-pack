# Scope Rubric

Quality checklist for `proposal/scope.md`.

---

## Must Criteria (all required to pass gate)

- [ ] **Objectives stated as outcomes.** Each objective is a measurable business outcome, not a task. "Reduce broker re-keying errors to zero" not "Implement an integration".
- [ ] **In-scope items listed as actions.** Each in-scope item is an action nterprise will take. Starts with a verb.
- [ ] **Out-of-scope explicitly stated.** At minimum 3 items are explicitly excluded. Silence is not exclusion.
- [ ] **Assumptions register present.** At minimum 2 assumptions are stated. Each assumption is falsifiable.
- [ ] **Success metrics defined.** At minimum 2 success metrics with specific thresholds.
- [ ] **No consulting-speak.** None of: leverage, utilise, synergise, holistic, seamless, robust, cutting-edge, innovative, value-add.

## Should Criteria (80% required)

- [ ] Objectives link to a named business driver from the intake
- [ ] Out-of-scope items reference why they are excluded (future phase, client responsibility, etc.)
- [ ] Assumptions assign a risk if the assumption proves false
- [ ] Success metrics specify measurement method (who measures, how, when)
- [ ] Section total length ≥ 400 words

## Minimum Word Count

400 words for the scope section.

## Structure

```markdown
## Objectives
[outcome-oriented statements]

## In Scope
[action-oriented bullets]

## Out of Scope
[explicit exclusions]

## Assumptions
[falsifiable assumptions with risk notes]

## Success Metrics
[specific, measurable criteria]
```
